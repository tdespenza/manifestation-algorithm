The Method

Your Development, Quantified

The Manifestation Algorithm turns honest self-reflection into measurable data you can track, improve, and compare over time.

I

Rate 40 Categories

Score yourself 1–10 across 40 distinct life categories — mindset, habits, relationships, spirituality, decision-making, and more. Sub-questions allow deeper precision where it matters.

II

Score Is Calculated

Each answer is multiplied by that category’s weight. Your total is the sum of all weighted ratings — a number between 1,000 and 10,000 reflecting your current alignment.

III

Track Your Progress

Complete the questionnaire weekly or monthly. The dashboard shows score history, category breakdowns, and trend charts — all stored locally on your device.

IV

Compare Anonymously

Opt in to anonymous P2P sharing via libp2p to see where your score stands globally — without sharing any personal information, email, or account.

Scoring Formula
Score = Σ (MaxPointsi × Ratingi / 10)

Each category carries a maximum point weight. You rate yourself 1–10 and earn a proportional share of that weight. Minimum score (all 1s) is 1,000. Maximum (all 10s) is 10,000.
